<h1 align="center">
    GDP Growth Indicators
</h1>

**GDP Growth Indicators** is a Victoria 3 mod that introduces GDP growth rate and ranking by it.

## Supported Version

1.9.8 - 1.12.3

## Supported Language

Brazilian Portuguese<br>
English<br>
French<br>
German<br>
Japanese<br>
Korean<br>
Polish<br>
Russian<br>
Simplified Chinese<br>
Spanish<br>
Turkish

## Indicators description:
1. Start-of-Year GDP. GDP at Jan 1 this year
2. Annual projected GDP growth rate. The estimated GDP growth this year
3. GDP growth rate this year. GDP growth from Jan 1 this year until now
